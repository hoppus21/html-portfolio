About Me

Hi, I'm Marek Záhorec, a web developer based in Vienna, Austria. Originally from the charming town of Trnava, Slovakia, my journey has been anything but conventional. After spending 15 years as a dedicated physiotherapist, I decided to follow my passion for technology and creativity by venturing into the world of coding. This bold career change has allowed me to channel my problem-solving skills and dedication into building dynamic, user-friendly web experiences.

My portfolio showcases a variety of web development projects, demonstrating my versatility and enthusiasm for crafting intuitive digital solutions. From sleek, responsive websites to complex web applications, I'm driven by a love for turning ideas into reality.

Beyond coding, I express my creativity in other ways. I’m the proud founder of a rock music project called Chapter 13, where I compose and produce original music. In addition to my musical pursuits, I also enjoy writing fiction, creating stories that reflect my imagination and love for storytelling.

Feel free to explore my portfolio, and don't hesitate to get in touch if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work. Thanks for visiting!
